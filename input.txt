Artificial intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are designed to think and act like humans. The term may also apply to any machine that exhibits traits associated with a human mind such as learning and problem-solving. AI is used in a variety of applications, including robotics, natural language processing, and self-driving cars. Over the past few years, AI has grown rapidly, with advancements in machine learning, deep learning, and neural networks. As AI continues to evolve, it is expected to play a major role in many industries, revolutionizing fields like healthcare, education, and manufacturing.
